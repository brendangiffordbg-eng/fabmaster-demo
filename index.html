FM-INSP · How it works

Four steps. Zero surprises.

Every piece of equipment on your site gets an asset tag, a schedule, and a paper trail. Downtime stops being a mystery.

STEP 01

Tag every asset

Each leg, conveyor, auger and platform gets a numbered Fabmaster asset tag and a documented baseline condition.

STEP 02

Inspect on schedule

Our certified crew runs your PMIs on a set cycle — safety, structure, wear parts, and vibration analysis where it counts.

STEP 03

Get the report

Photos, findings, and plain-language recommendations land in your customer portal — every inspection, every asset, forever.

STEP 04

Repair before failure

Issues get quoted and fixed on your schedule — in the off-season, not mid-harvest with a line of trucks waiting.

FM-SAFE · What we inspect

Safety compliance, in writing.

Our staff are trained in working at heights, confined space, lockout/tagout, WHMIS, aerial lift and fall arrest — and every inspection covers the items your insurer and the Ministry care about.

  • Machine guardingBelts, drives, pinch points — guarded, secured, documented.
  • Structural conditionLegs, towers, supports and catwalks checked for corrosion, cracking and fatigue.
  • Ladders & platformsAccess equipment verified against current safety standards.
  • Fall arrest pointsAnchors and tie-offs inspected and logged per asset.
  • Vibration analysisBearings and drives screened so failures show up in a report, not at 2 a.m.
